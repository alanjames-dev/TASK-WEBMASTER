About The Event

KITeS 2021

The much awaited technical extravaganza is finally here! Yes, it's time for Kerala IEEE Technical Exhibition and Symposium (KITeS) 2021! We have in store for you a whole range of technical marvel tailored perfectly to give you the best!

IEEE LINK

IEEE LINK (Local Integrated Network of IEEE Students) is an innovative concept that originated in IEEE Kerala Section, aimed at networking student branches in an effective way, thereby increasing the value in IEEE Student membership.
